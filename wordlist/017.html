RTH 1 religion A set of beliefs and practices agreed upon by a number of people. religious Of or about religion; someone who is very devout to their religion. protestant Any Western Christian who is not Catholic, Anglican, or Eastern Church. moral Relating to the principles of what is right and wrong. work ethic A belief in the moral benefit and importance of work and its ability to strengthen who you are as a person. volunteer A person who performs a service without pay. philanthropist A person who is concerned about human welfare and gives time and money to help people. missionary A person sent by a church to teach about the religion and do philanthropic work. religious pluralism The idea that people from different religious backgrounds can live together peacefully. tolerance A permissive attitude toward those whose beliefs and opinions and different from one's own.
